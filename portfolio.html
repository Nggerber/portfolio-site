Portfolio

Utah's Snapshot is an outdoor recreation appliction developed by Neal Gerber, Christian Boswell and Sydney Goodwill. It is designed to be a full service recreation app for some of Utah's most popular destinations. The application displays weather information and outdoor activities to do in eight of Utah's top outdoor destinations.
